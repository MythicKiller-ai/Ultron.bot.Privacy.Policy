Privacy Policy

Effective as of January 1, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Ultron™. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your data while you interact with our Discord bot. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Data Collection

Ultron™ may collect the following information:

  • Discord user ID and username
  • Server ID and channel information
  • Message content for features like AFK, economy, giveaways, and moderation logs
  • Bot usage statistics and command interactions

Data Usage

  • Provide bot commands and features
  • Server moderation and logging
  • Track user stats, economy balances, and game results
  • Improve bot performance and develop new features

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Limited sharing occurs only for bot functionality (e.g., logging moderation actions, displaying leaderboards) and only within the server where the data was generated. No data is shared with third parties except as required by law.

Data Security

We implement strong measures to protect your data from unauthorised access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. Users are responsible for managing permissions and shared data within their own servers.

User Control

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of certain bot features (e.g., economy tracking, AFK) by not using those commands
  • Request deletion of your server's data by contacting the developer
  • Review the data we hold about you

Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of any significant changes via our support server. Continued use of Ultron™ indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
